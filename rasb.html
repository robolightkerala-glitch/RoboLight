Board Specifications

Processor (CPU)

Broadcom Quad-Core ARM Cortex Architecture delivering high performance processing power for complex robotics algorithms.

Memory (RAM)

Available in LPDDR4 configurations up to 8GB, enabling computer vision, AI tasks, and real-time path planning.

Connectivity

Dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.0, and Gigabit Ethernet for seamless remote control, telemetry, and IoT integration.

Display & Camera

Dual micro-HDMI outputs supporting 4K resolution, plus MIPI CSI/DSI camera ports for vision systems.

Robotics & Interface Capability

40-Pin GPIO Header

The heart of all ROBOLIGHT hardware integrations. Connect sensors, motor drivers, and actuators with ease.

  • PWM Control: Drive servos and DC motor drivers smoothly.
  • I2C Bus: Interface with IMUs, accelerometers, and OLED displays.
  • SPI & UART: High-speed sensor communications and serial links.
  • 5V & 3.3V Power Pins: Direct power rails for lightweight electronics modules.
